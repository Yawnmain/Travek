Travek is a project created by ISU students to defend a project in English. Our project helps in choosing a country for travel.

For example, we have preferences that we want to take into account when traveling, but simply do not know anything about countries, then this service will help with the choice.

In the future, if everything goes well, then maybe it can be expanded and scaled, but for now it will exist in this form
